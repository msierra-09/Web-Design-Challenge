Comparisons

Cities in the dataset experience humidity between 0% and 100%. There are a few cities in the Northern Hemisphere where the humidity is 0%. While there is no one-to-one association between the humidity and latitudes, there is a somewhat symmetrical distribution of cities in both hemispheres for humidity between 60% and 100%, and between 20% and 60%.
